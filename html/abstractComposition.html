9" x 9"
Graphic Design, Composition.

I cut and pasted Circles and Lines of varying sizes to form abstract compositions that could be interpreted in many different ways.

After choosing nine words, I designed a series of word communications. The compositions visually communicate the meanings of the words chosen through dots of varying sizes.

On choosing a typeface (Didot), I cropped four different letterforms to create and abstraction that reveals the individual characteristics and examines the construction of the letterforms.
