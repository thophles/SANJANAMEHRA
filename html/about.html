ABOUT ME


As a Chicago based designer, I work towards creating bold statements with a minimalist aesthetic. I am drawn to typographic design that emphasizes the abstraction in letterforms along with following subtle optical laws to invoke curiosity. I believe that I have yet to produce my best works which is what motivates me to challenge myself at every step throughout my design process.
