ABOUT


As a Chicago based designer, Sanjana works towards creating bold statements with a minimalist aesthetic. She is drawn to typographic design that emphasizes the abstraction in letterform and follows subtle optical laws which invoke curiosity. Sanjana believes that she is yet to produce her best works which is her motivation and challenge at every step throughout her design journey.
