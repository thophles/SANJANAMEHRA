Graphic Design, Magazine.

Untitled Magazine is a bi-annual collaborative publication designed by a group of students from the Visual Communication Design Department at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago. The objective was to provide a platform for students to be able to feature their various forms of work. The theme of a computer desktop screen was used to define the typographic and design decisions throughout the seventh issue.
