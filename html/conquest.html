Graphic Design, Game Board.

A short story by Jorge Borges was the inspiration for developing the visual metaphor to retell the story of the rise and fall of an empire. Taking cue from the game “Risk” a series of five game boards was designed to geographically show the rise and fall of the British empire in India.
