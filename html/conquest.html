Graphic Design, Game Board.

A short story by Jorge Borges served as the basis for developing a visual metaphor to retell the story of the rise and fall of an empire. Inspired by the game “Risk” I designed a series of 5 game boards to show the geographical rise and fall of the British Empire in India.
