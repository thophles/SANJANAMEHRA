24" x 36"
Graphic Design, Poster.

World Music Wednesdays is an announcement poster for a weekly folk music concert hosted by the Old Town School of Folk Music. The treatment of the letterforms in the title combined with the graphic elements that represent guitar strings and a fretboard give the poster a musical movement while the structure created by the text along with the dates aims to create a sense of unity of elements similar to the unity of different cultures that takes place at these events. The color chosen is warm and bright and highlights the concept of festivity and cultural unity.
