Brochure Design

This type specimen brochure has everything you need to know about the typeface Bodoni! The brochure expresses the unique characteristics, history and uses of the typeface following Massimo Vignelli's style of using the color palette of red, black and white. Massimo Vignelli was an Italian designer who admired the elegance in the characteristics of Bodoni and used it frequently in his works.
