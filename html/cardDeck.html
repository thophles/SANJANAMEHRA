2" x 5"
Graphic Design, Cards.

Created a deck of 10 transparent cards with a common theme, X-ray. The cards provide information on elements in the periodic table, used in X-ray scans for students learning about the periodic table. The transparency of the cards further emphasizes that they must be held up in the air or placed on a light box in order to be legible, similar to the treatment of actual X-ray scans.
