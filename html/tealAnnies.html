4" x 9"
Package Design, Box.

Redesigned the packaging of Annie's Bunny Tails, Mac and Cheese. Based on research, Annie's Bunny Tails Mac and Cheese is mostly purchased by high income, Caucasian and middle-age women shopping for their children. The teal color aims to attract the attention of women while the addition of a game and playful aspects to the design, aims to keep the children busy as they wait eagerly for their mac and cheese!
